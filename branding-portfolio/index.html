Brand & Identity Design - The Whole Bean

The Whole Bean Style Guide

The Whole Bean brand style guide includes the mission statement, brand personality, color scheme, typography guidelines, logo, iconography, image guidelines and social media headers for a local coffee shop.

The Whole Bean Landing Page

The Whole Bean landing page is the introduction of the coffee shop to the community and its target audience. It states promotional items for its grand opening and encourages people to sign up for its newsletter.

The Whole Bean Design Presentation

The Whole Bean Design Presentation is part of the iteration process for the landing page design. Introducing the shop, landing page and key goals to their target audience, I received valuable feedback. I looked for trends and standout responses and incorporated those changes to make a solid landing page design.
